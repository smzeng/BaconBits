What We Do

Every year hundreds of thousands of livestock and wild animals are killed by infectious diseases. African Swine Fever, for example, kills tens of thousands of pigs across Europe and Asia. We designed this website as a way to help farmers keep track of their pigs' health. By inputing their pigs' data into the database, they are able to figure out whether their pigs are susceptible to AFS.
